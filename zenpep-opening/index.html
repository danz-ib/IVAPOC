Important Safety Information

ZENPEP

Pancrelipase Delayed-Release Capsules

Indication

ZENPEP is indicated for the treatment of exocrine pancreatic insufficiency due to cystic fibrosis or other conditions.

Dosage

Individualize dosage based on clinical symptoms, the degree of steatorrhea present, and the fat content of the diet.

Important Safety Information

  • Fibrosing colonopathy has been reported with high-dose pancreatic enzyme replacement therapy
  • Use with caution in patients with gout, renal impairment, or hyperuricemia
  • Most common adverse reactions include headache, abdominal pain, and flatulence
  • Safety and effectiveness in children below 1 year of age have not been established
